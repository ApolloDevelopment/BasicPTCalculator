  • Find Hypotenuse
    Input the value of the A and B legs to get the length of leg C.

    Syntax: getHypotenuse(a, b);
    —Example—
    getHypotenuse(3, 4);
    Output: 5
  • Find Missing Leg
    Input the value of the B (or A) and C legs to get the length of leg A (or B).

    Syntax: getMissingLeg(b, c);
    —Example—
    getMissingLeg(4, 5);
    Output: 3
  • Triangle Identification
    States whether the triangle is obtuse, acute, or right based on the side lengths given to the function.

    Syntax: getRelation(a, b, c);
    —Example—
    getRelation(3, 4, 5);
    Output: "Right"
  • Solve a (Complex) Radical
    Gets the final value of a complex radical.

    Syntax: radical(a, b);
    —Example—
    radical(2, 2);
    Output: 8
